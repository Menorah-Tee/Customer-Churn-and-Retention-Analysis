# Customer-Churn-and-Retention-Analysis
 This project Analyzes customer churn behaviour and Customer vLifetime Value (CLV) to understand customer retention patterns, revenue loss, and key drivers of churn across different customer segments.
 
🔗[View Dataset on GitHub](https://github.com/Menorah-Tee/Customer-Churn-and-Retention-Analysis) 

## 🗂 Project Summary
Customer retention is critical to business growth and profitability. This Excel dashboard provides insights into:
. Customer churn patterns
. Revenue loss due to churn
. Customer segmentation by risk and value
. Behavioral drivers influencing churn
. Distribution of churn across customer groups

## 📌 Executive Summary
The analysis reveals an overall churn rate of 31%, indicating moderate customer attrition. While high-value customers are largely retained, churn is primarily concentrated among lower-value and high-risk segments.
A total estimated CLV loss of $36.95M highlights the financial impact of churn. Key drivers of churn include low customer engagement, high-risk profiles, and service-related factors such as resolution time.

## 🏭 Industry Context
Customer churn analysis is essential in data-driven businesses to:
. Improve customer retention and loyalty
. Reduce revenue loss
. Identify high-value customers
. Optimize customer experience
. Support strategic decision-making

## 🔍 Key Insights & Recommendations
 1. Churn Drivers
Low engagement customers show the highest churn, while service experience (e.g., slow resolution time) increases churn likelihood.
 → Recommendations:
Improve customer engagement through personalized communication and faster support response times.
 2. Customer Risk Segmentation
High-risk customers are more likely to churn, though they contribute less overall revenue.
 → Recommendations:
Implement targeted retention strategies for high-risk segments using incentives and engagement campaigns.
 3. Customer Value Distribution (CLV)
The majority of revenue is generated by low-risk, high-value customers, with no high-value customers in the high-risk segment.
→ Recommendations:
Maintain loyalty programs and proactive engagement to retain high-value customers.
4. Revenue Impact of Churn
Churn leads to a significant total CLV loss despite similar average CLV between active and churned customers.
→ Recommendations:
Focus on early detection of churn signals to reduce long-term revenue loss.
5. Customer Segmentation Insights
Churn is influenced by multiple factors including engagement, service experience, and behavioral risk indicators rather than a single variable.
→ Recommendations:
Adopt a multi-factor approach to churn prediction and customer retention strategies.
